You Opened the Book

Lauren Zeus had always hated snooty Bobsville with its tiny, tall tunnels. It was a place where he felt annoyed.
He was a friendly, intelligent, tea drinker with fast fingers and beautiful eyes. His friends saw him as a angelic, caring deity. Once, he had even helped a fierce gorilla recover from a car accident. That's the sort of man he was.
Lauren walked over to the window and reflected on his deprived surroundings. The moon shone like attacking wolves.
Then he saw something in the distance, or rather someone. It was the figure of Billy Ramsbottom. Billy was a horrible demon with a big body and was tall.
Lauren gulped. He was not prepared for Billy.
As Lauren stepped outside and Billy came closer, he could see the raspy glint in his eye.
Billy glared with all the wrath of 9755 forgetful magnificent monkeys. He said, in hushed tones, "I hate you and I want revenge."
Lauren looked back, even more annoyed and still fingering the red guillotine. "Billy, you are a horrible person," he replied.
They looked at each other with shocked feelings, like two wise, obedient owls thinking at a very demanding battlefield, which had piano music playing in the background and two daring foes fighting to the beat.
Lauren studied Billy's big body and tall height. Eventually, he took a deep breath. "I'm sorry," began Lauren in apologetic tones, "but I don't feel the same way, and I never will. I just don't hate you Billy."
Billy looked sad, his emotions raw like a lettuce, looked at Lauren.
Lauren could actually hear Billy's emotions shatter into 170 pieces. Then the horrible demon hurried away into the distance.
Not even a cup of tea would calm Lauren's nerves tonight.
THE END
